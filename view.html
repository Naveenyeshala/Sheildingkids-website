About Us

Welcome to Sheildingkids, where our mission is to ensure that every child, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to thrive and succeed. Founded 30 years ago, we have grown into a global force for change, impacting the lives of children in 5 countries across worldwide. Our vision is a world where every child's potential is realized, their dreams are nurtured, and their rights are protected. We achieve this by providing essential support and resources, including education, healthcare, nutrition, and emotional well-being, guided by the principles of compassion, integrity, and innovation. Our goals include expanding access to quality education, improving healthcare outcomes, addressing malnutrition, promoting emotional well-being, and fostering sustainable change through strategic partnerships and community development projects. What sets us apart is our holistic approach to child welfare, innovative solutions, community-centric focus, and commitment to transparency and accountability. Join us in our mission to build a brighter future for children worldwide, where every child has the opportunity to thrive and succeed.
