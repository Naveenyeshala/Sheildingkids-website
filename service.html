Our Services

In our Sheildingkids, there is a nurturing space where young minds play, study, and explore in a supportive environment. Here, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to uplift and empower children from underprivileged backgrounds. From educational programs to recreational activities, we provide a holistic approach to child development. Our services include:

  • Educational Support: We offer tutoring, homework assistance, and access to educational materials to help children excel academically.
  • Healthcare Services: Our center provides basic healthcare services, including medical check-ups, vaccinations,and health education sessions to promote overall well-being.
  • Nutritional Support: We ensure that children have access to nutritious meals and snacks to support their growth and development.
  • Recreational Activities: Through playtime, sports, and creative arts programs, we encourage children to explore their interests and develop essential life skills.
  • Counseling and Emotional Support: Our trained counselors provide emotional support and guidance to children facing challenging circumstances, helping them build resilience and coping strategies.

Explore our photo gallery to witness the joy and excitement that fills our center as children participate in our diverse range of programs. Join us in our mission to provide a safe and stimulating space where every child can flourish and reach their full potential.
