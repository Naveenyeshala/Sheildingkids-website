For decades, the Child and Old Age Welfare Trust has been devoted to uplifting the lives of the most vulnerable children and elderly individuals. Through online donations, we extend vital support to disadvantaged children, women, and low-income communities, offering improved healthcare, educational opportunities, and safeguarding against physical and mental exploitation. Embedded within impoverished communities, our development projects prioritize restoring the innocence of childhood, rescuing children from the clutches of malnutrition, facilitating access to education, ensuring safe pregnancies for women, and liberating children from exploitative labor. Our achievements are indebted to the compassionate contributions of countless donors. With your continued support, we strive to perpetuate our mission with unwavering vigor and compassion.

WHY DONATE?

Your invaluable donation powers our mission to support the elderly and children in need. With your generosity, we provide crucial resources like education, nutrition, healthcare, and protection from abuse and exploitation. Without your unwavering support, our efforts would come to a standstill. It takes each and every one of us joining forces to ensure a brighter and more hopeful future for both the elderly and children worldwide
